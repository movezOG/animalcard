Cheetah

During a hunt the cheetah trips its pray then bite it on the throat to suffocate it.

  • Specific Name:Acinonyx Jubatus
  • Average Length: 1.1 - 1.5m
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 Years
  • Speed: 110-120km/h
  • Habitat: Sub-Saharan Africa

The Cheetah is a large cat of the subfamily felinae that occurs southern, Southern, North and East Africa, and a few localities in Iran.
